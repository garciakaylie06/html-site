    Projects as a Learning Process

    I view projects as a way to practice thinking, not just building. Each project helps me understand how small decisions—such as naming headings or choosing semantic elements—affect the overall clarity of a page. This site itself is one of those projects.

    Many of my projects involve writing instructions, creating structured content, or organizing information for an audience. I enjoy breaking down tasks into steps and making sure the final result is easy to follow. These skills are especially important in technical and professional environments.

    As I continue developing this site, I plan to expand this section with more detailed project descriptions. I want to include not only what I built, but what I learned, what challenged me, and how I improved. That reflection is just as important as the finished product.
