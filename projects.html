    Projects as a Learning Process

    I see projects as a way to practice thinking, not just building things. Every project teaches me how small choices, like naming headings or picking semantic elements, can change how clear a page is overall.

    A lot of the work I do involves writing instructions, making structured content, or putting information in order for an audience. I like breaking things down into steps and making sure the end result is easy to understand. These skills are very important in professional and technical settings.

    As I keep working on this site, I plan to add more detailed project descriptions to this part. I want to include not only what I made, but also what I learned, what was hard for me, and how I got better. That thought is just as important as the end result.
